Uploading and Installing Hero

To install your theme in WordPress you must navigate to the menu "Appearance > Themes". Now click the "Install Themes" tab and then the text button at the top "Upload".

You then should click Choose File and find your theme files on your computer and upload them and then after the upload is finished "Activate" your theme.

Just to be easier you should upload the installable only archive you donwload from ThemeForest

This should pretty much do it - meaning you have installed Hero and now you need to install the recomanded plugins.
